# Finance Manager

Welcome to the Finance Manager App! This Android application, built using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, allows you to manage your finances efficiently. With a user-friendly interface and robust features, tracking your payments and expenses has never been easier.

## Features
### 1. Payment Management
- **Add, update and delete payments**: Seamlessly manage your payments by adding new ones, updating existing ones, and removing unwanted ones with ease. Attach detailed information and pictures for reference.
- **Sort and filter expenses:** Multiple sorting and filtering criteria such as date, amount, or category allow you to easily find and manage all the payments.

### 2. Graphical Insights
- **Payment breakdown graph**: Visualize your spending habits with an intuitive graph that displays the breakdown of payments based on categories. Gain valuable insights into where your money is going and make informed financial decisions.

### 3. Backend Integration with Firebase
- **Secure user profiles**: Your user profile is securely managed with Firebase backend integration, ensuring that your personal data is protected and your privacy is respected.
- **Different login methods:** The app introduces multiple different login methods to allow you to choose the one most fitting for your needs. You can use Google, Facebook and GitHub to sign in, or just simply register the classic way by providing email and password.
- **Cloud sync**: Enjoy the convenience of cloud sync, allowing your data to effortlessly synchronize across all your devices.
